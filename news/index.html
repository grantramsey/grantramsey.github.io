Announcements

  • Grant Ramsey will give a keynote address at the Culture Conference 2017.
  • We recently organized a conference on The Science of Evolution and the Evolution of the sciences at KU Leuven.
  • The Ramsey Lab had just moved to Belgium! Grant Ramsey accepted a position as BOFZAP Research Professor at the Institute of Philosophy at KU Leuven, beginning summer 2016. He is currently seeking postdocs and graduate students - please email him if you are interested.
  • Grant Ramsey was awarded a sabbatical at the National Humanities Center and was in residence August 2015 - May 2016.
  • Grant Ramsey was appointed as a research fellow at the Helsinki Collegium for Advanced Studies from 1 September 2015 to 31 July 2017. (Declined for National Humanities Center fellowship and KU Leuven BOFZAP Research Professorship.)
  • Grant Ramsey was awarded a fellowship at the Pittsburgh Center for Philosophy of Science for the 2015 - 2016 academic year. (Declined for National Humanities Center fellowship.)
  • Charles Pence and Grant Ramsey were awarded a National Science Foundation Scholars Award for The evoText Project.
  • The evoText Project recently received some press in NDWorks.
  • Michael Deem accepted a tenure track job at Indiana State University where he will be Assistant Professor of Philosophy and an affiliated faculty member in The Center for Genomic Advocacy.
  • Michael Deem accepted a Pediatric Bioethics and Genomics Postdoctoral Fellowship at Children’s Mercy Hospitals and Clinics for the 2015 - 2016 academic year.
